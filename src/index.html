Average number of nearby jobs declines in South King County
Between 2000 and 2012, the average number of jobs within a typical commute distance* in Seattle went up 1.4 percent. On the Eastside, it increased 5.1 percent. In South King County, though, the average number of nearby jobs went down 5.1 percent.
*Number of jobs that fall within a 9-mile radius of the center of each census tract
